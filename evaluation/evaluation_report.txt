[METRIC DEFINITIONS]
  MAE     – Mean Absolute Error (avg prediction error in score points)
  RMSE    – Root Mean Squared Error (larger errors penalised more)
  R²      – Coefficient of determination (1.0 = perfect fit)
  Exact%  – % essays with predicted integer score == true score
  ±1 Acc% – % essays predicted within ±1 score point of truth
